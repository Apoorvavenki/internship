About Microbes

Microbes are tiny living things that are found all around us and are too small to be seen by the naked eye. They live in water, soil, and in the air. The human body is home to millions of these microbes too, also called microorganisms. Microbes come in a huge variety of shapes - everything from rods to spheres, even corkscrew shapes. They are all about the same size, with some a little bigger than others and smaller than others.
